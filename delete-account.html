Delete Account – VAGUS AI

Users may request deletion of their VAGUS AI account and associated data at any time.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the VAGUS AI app
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Select “Delete Account”
  4. Confirm your request

If you cannot access your account, you may email:

ooumaruno1@gmail.com

What happens when you delete your account

  • Your profile information is permanently deleted
  • Your associated surgery logs are deleted
  • Your authentication credentials are removed

Some limited data may be retained for up to 30 days for backup and security purposes.
